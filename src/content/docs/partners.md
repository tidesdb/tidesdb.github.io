### MariaDB Foundation
<a target="_blank" href="https://mariadb.org/"><img src="/partners/mariadb-logo.png" alt="MariaDB Logo" width="200" height="100"></a>

<a target="_blank" href="https://mariadb.org/">MariaDB Foundation</a> is an independent non-profit organization that promotes, protects, and advances the open-source MariaDB database and its community. TidesDB is proud to be a Silver Sponsor and technology partner of the MariaDB Foundation, collaborating with the MariaDB community to advance open-source database technology through TideSQL, the pluggable storage engine that brings TidesDB’s capabilities to MariaDB.
